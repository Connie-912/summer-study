知识精讲

状语从句在复合句中修饰主句中的动词、形容词或副词，表示时间、条件、原因、让步、目的、结果、比较、方式等。掌握各类状语从句的引导词是解题关键。

类型常见引导词例句特别提示
时间when, while, as, before, after, since, until, as soon as, the momentI was reading when he came in.主将从现：时间状语从句用一般现在时表将来
条件if, unless, as long as, on condition thatIf it rains tomorrow, I will stay at home.主将从现；unless=if...not
原因because, since, as, now thatHe was late because he got up late.because语气最强，回答why；since/as表示已知原因
让步although, though, even though, even if, while, no matter wh-,-everAlthough he is young, he knows a lot.although/though不能与but连用，但可与yet/still连用
目的so that, in order that, for fear that, in caseHe got up early so that he could catch the bus.目的状语从句常用情态动词can/could/may/might
结果so...that..., such...that...He is so kind that everyone likes him.so+adj./adv.；such+n.；so many/much+n.
比较than, as...as..., not so/as...as..., the more...the more...He runs faster than I (do).比较对象要一致；the more...是高频more...the考点
 though后的从句可as, as if, as as if/as方式Do as I told you.用虚拟语气though 
 典例破题 
 【例1】I (用适当的连词填空) will call you______I arrive in Beijing.解析：主句用将来时，从句表示"一……就……"，用as when。注意soon as/the moment/从句用一般现在时。 
 【例2】______he is very old, he still works hard every day.解析：让步状语从句，表示"尽管"，用Although though。注意不能与but连/Though/Even用。 
 【例3】It was______a difficult problem______nobody could solve it.解析：结果状语从句，"如此……以至于……"，problem是名词，用such...that...。so...that...用于形容词/副词前。 
 巩固提升 
 【巩固题】用适当的连词填空：1. You won't pass the exam______you study harder. 2. He speaks English______he were an American. 3. The______you practice, the______you will become. 4.______it was raining heavily, they still went on working. 5. He didn't go to school yesterday______he was ill. 
参考答案：

1. unless —— 条件状语从句，unless = if...not，意为"除非"

2. as if / as though —— 方式状语从句，因与事实相反（他不是美国人），用虚拟语气were

3. more; better —— the + 比较级, the + 比较级，比较状语从句，"越……越……"

4. Although / Though / Even though —— 让步状语从句，表示"尽管/虽然"

 5.——原因状语从句，回答为什么没去上学because
 易错警示
 ⚠易错点：·主将从现：在时间状语从句和条件状语从句中，用一般现在时表示将来。如：If it rains home.（rain用一般现在时）tomorrow, I will stay at·although与but不能连用：英语中一个句子只能有一个连词引导从句。同样，because与so也不能连用。·such...that...：so修饰形容词/副词，such修饰名词。但要注意：so so...that... vs that。many/much/few/little+n.+·until的用法：not...until...（直到……才……）；延续性动词+until；瞬间动词+not...until。
