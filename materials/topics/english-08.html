知识精讲

应用文写作是高考英语的必考题型（通常占15-25分），考查学生在真实语境中运用英语的能力。本专题提供六大常见体裁的写作模板和实用句型，基础薄弱的同学可直接背诵套用。

一、书信（Letter）

通用结构：

· 开头：Dear ___, / I'm writing to... / How is everything going?· 正文：First of all... / What's more... / Last but not least...· 结尾：I'm looking forward to your reply. / Best wishes! / Yours sincerely,

二、通知（Notice）

通用结构：

· 标题：NOTICE（居中大写）· 正文：In order to... / ...will be held... / It will start at... / Everyone is welcome.· 落款：Students' Union / The School Office（右下角）· 日期：落款上方

三、演讲稿（Speech）

通用结构：

· 开头：Good morning, everyone! / It's a great honor to... / I'm glad to standhere to...· 正文：The topic of my speech is... / Firstly... Secondly... / For example...· 结尾：That's all. Thank you for listening! / Let's take action now!

 四、建议信（Letter Advice）of
 万能句型：·I'm sorry to hear that.../You asked for my advice about.../Here are some suggestions.
 ·It would be a good idea to.../Why not...?/You'd better.../I suggest that you should...·I hope my suggestions will be helpful./I'm sure you will make it.
 五、感谢信（Letter Thanks）of
 万能句型：·I'm writing to express my sincere thanks to you for.../I'm truly grateful for...·Without your help, I couldn't have.../It was your kindness that made it possible...·Again, I would like to express my warm thanks to you./I will never forget your kindness.
 六、邀请信（Letter Invitation）of
 万能句型：·I'm writing to invite you to.../It's my pleasure to invite you to...·The activity will be held in... on.../It will start at... and last for about...·I would appreciate it if you could come./I'm looking forward to your early reply.

典例破题
【例1】假设你是李华，你校将举办英语演讲比赛，请你写一封邀请信邀请外教Mr.

Smith担任评委。

参考范文：Dear Mr. Smith,I'm writing to invite you to be a judge for the English speech contest, which will be heldin the school hall at 2:00 p.m. next Friday. The contest will last for about two hours. As an experienced teacher, your opinion will be of great value to us. I would appreciateit if you could accept our invitation.I'm looking forward to your early reply. Yours sincerely,Li Hua

【例2】假设你是李华，你的朋友Tom来信说他学习压力大，请你写一封建议信。

参考范文：Dear Tom,I'm sorry to hear that you are under great pressure from your studies. Here are somesuggestions that may help you. First of all, you'd better make a reasonable study plan and follow it strictly. What's more,why not take regular exercise? It can help you relax both physically and mentally. Lastbut not least, you should talk to your teachers or friends when you feel stressed.I hope my suggestions will be helpful to you. Yours sincerely,Li Hua

巩固提升
【巩固题】写作微练习：

1. 翻译下列写作常用句型：

1. 我写信是想邀请你参加我们的活动。

2. 我真的很感激你在我困难的时候给予我的帮助。

3. 如果你能接受我的邀请，我将不胜感激。

4. 在我看来，学习英语最重要的是坚持。

5. 首先，你应该制定一个合理的学习计划。

2. 假设你是学生会主席李华，请用英语写一则通知，告知全校学生本周五下午3点在学校礼堂将举办一场英语学习方法讲座。

参考答案：

1. 翻译答案：

1. I'm writing to invite you to take part in our activity.

2. I really appreciate your help when I was in trouble/difficulty.

3. I would appreciate it if you could accept my invitation.

4. In my opinion / As far as I'm concerned, the most important thing in learningEnglish is persistence.

5. First of all, you should make a reasonable study plan.

2. 通知范文：

NOTICE

In order to help students improve their English, a lecture on English learningmethods will be held in the school hall at 3:00 p.m. this Friday. An experienced English teacher will share useful tips on how to learn Englishmore effectively. Everyone is welcome to attend. Please be on time and bring a notebook with you. Students' UnionJuly 20, 2026

易错警示
⚠ 易错点：

· 格式错误：书信要有称呼、正文、结束语和签名；通知要有标题（NOTICE）、正文、落款和日期。格式分很关键！

· 时态混乱：描述将要发生的事用一般将来时，描述过去的事用一般过去时，描述客观事实用一般现在时。

· 中式英语：避免逐字翻译中文。如"我很高兴"不能写成"I am very happy"（太口语化），应用"I'm delighted/pleased to..."。

· 字数不足：高考应用文一般要求80-100词，太少会扣分。建议背熟模板，考试时套用即可。

· 拼写和标点：拼写错误会扣分，尤其是常见词（如receive不是recieve）。英文标点与中文不同，不要混用。
